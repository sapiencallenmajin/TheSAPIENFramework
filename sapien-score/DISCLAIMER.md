# Disclaimer

## Cultural References

"Voigt-Kampff" is a reference to the Voigt-Kampff test from Philip K. Dick's novel "Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?" (1968) and its film adaptation "Blade Runner" (1982). This project is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Philip K. Dick estate, Warner Bros., or any related rights holders. The name is used as a cultural allusion to describe the tool's function of evaluating AI behavioral authenticity.
